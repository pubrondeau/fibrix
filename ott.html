What is an OTT Bundle?

OTT (Over-the-Top) bundles let you access premium streaming platforms like Netflix, Disney+ Hotstar, SonyLIV, Zee5, and more — all included with your Fibrix broadband plan. No separate subscriptions, no extra bills.

FAQs

  • How do I activate my OTT bundle? Activation instructions are sent by SMS/email after signup. Most platforms use your mobile number for login.
  • Can I use my own existing accounts? OTT bundles are tied to your broadband plan and may require new logins.
  • Is there a lock-in period? No lock-in for monthly plans. Yearly plans offer better value.
