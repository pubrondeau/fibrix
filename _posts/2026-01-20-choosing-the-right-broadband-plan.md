With so many broadband options available, picking the right plan can feel overwhelming. At Fibrix Broadband, we've designed our plans to be simple, transparent, and value-packed. Here's a quick guide to help you choose the perfect plan for your household.

## Understanding Internet Speed

Internet speed is measured in **Mbps (Megabits per second)**. The higher the number, the faster your connection. Here's a rough guide:

| Speed | Best For |
| --- | --- |
| 30–40 Mbps | Basic browsing, email, social media, SD streaming |
| 60 Mbps | HD streaming, video calls, moderate gaming, 3–4 devices |
| 80 Mbps | 4K streaming, online gaming, work from home, 5+ devices |
| 100 Mbps | Heavy usage, large file downloads, multiple 4K streams, smart home devices |

## How to Decide

1. **Count your devices.** More devices = higher speed needed.
2. **Check your streaming habits.** 4K streaming needs 25+ Mbps per stream.
3. **Consider OTT.** If you're paying for streaming services separately, an OTT combo plan could save you money.
4. **Think about duration.** If you're sure about staying, yearly plans offer the best value.
